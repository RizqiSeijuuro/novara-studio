FAQ

Fair questions, honest answers.

Why fixed pricing instead of hourly?+

Hourly billing punishes efficiency and creates anxiety on both sides. You know exactly what you're paying and exactly what you're getting. If it takes me longer, that's my problem — not your invoice.

Do you write the copy, or do I provide it?+

I write it. Design and copy are inseparable on a landing page — a beautiful page with weak words doesn't convert. You review and approve everything before build.

Why Framer and not Webflow or custom code?+

Framer gives you agency-quality design with zero maintenance burden — you can edit text and images yourself without breaking anything. Strong preference for another stack? Ask me; Brand Site projects are flexible.

What if I need changes after the revision rounds?+

Small tweaks within 7 days of launch are always free. Bigger changes run through the Keep Improving plan or a separate quote — always agreed before work starts.

What timezone are you in? Will communication be slow?+

I work async-first. You get a daily written progress update and responses within 12 hours. Most clients tell me I communicate better than agencies in their own city.

What do you need from me to start?+

Your product (or a demo), your target audience, and 45 minutes for the kickoff call. That's it — I handle the rest.
